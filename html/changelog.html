SkyratBot updated:

  • skateboards and wheelys dont move multiple tiles anymore
  • removes a runtime when ahealing moths
  • CE now properly spawns with an engineering skillchip.
  • Fixed floorbots applying floor tiles onto already tiled turfs.
  • Fixed floorbot issues with material tiles.

coiax updated:

  • Regular Capture-The-Flag players can no longer be wounded. Simulated participants in the Medieval Shuttle can still be violently murdered with wounds however.
  • Job specific benefits around food and drink have been moved into the liver. For example, a security officer starts with a liver that gains more of a benefit from donuts and Quad Sec. Steal that liver, and digest donuts like a cop!
  • Medical Doctors, the CMO and the chaplain have a new skillchip, allowing them to infer a person's career choice from examining their liver. Observers also have access to this knowledge.
  • Livers from clowns, clownops and bananium golems now honk when squeezed.
  • Fixed an oversight where clownops couldn't see each other as syndicate agents.
  • Fixed the clown operative leader not having the same traits as other clownops.
  • Fixed being able to ignore some caltrops through walking.
  • Runtime Station now has a small nanite lab, south of the construction area.
